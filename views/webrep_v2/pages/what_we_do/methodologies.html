Methodologies

Hub-and-Spoke
Model

The RAPID Growth Project adopts a Hub-and-Spoke Model to strengthen coordination and efficiency across agricultural value chains. By linking smallholder farmers and producer groups (spokes) to established enterprises and market actors (hubs), the model facilitates aggregation, improves market access, and enables the delivery of targeted support services, financing, and infrastructure. This helps create a more connected, competitive, and inclusive rural enterprise system.

Value Chain
Market-driven Approach

The project adopts a market-driven approach by aligning its interventions with existing demand and market opportunities within selected value chains. By strengthening linkages between producers, enterprises, and buyers, the project ensures that production, investments, and support services respond to real market needs. This enhances competitiveness, reduces risks, and improves income opportunities for farmers and agri-based enterprises.
