About the Project

The Rural Agro-enterprise Partnership for Inclusive Development and Growth (RAPID Growth) Project is a flagship initiative of the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) funded by the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) that aims to strengthen agro-enterprise development in rural communities across the Philippines.

Aligned with the Philippine Development Plan (PDP) 2017-2022, the project seeks to expand economic opportunities for farming households, cooperatives, and microenterprises by promoting inclusive and sustainable value chains in the agriculture sector.

The RAPID Growth Project supports DTI's development thrust of Trabaho, Negosyo, at Kabuhayan (TNK) by helping generate employment, build viable enterprises, and improve livelihoods in rural areas. Through a Value Chain Approach, the project works to strengthen linkages between producers, markets, and service providers to enhance productivity, competitiveness, and market access.

The project also adopts a Whole-of-Government Approach (WGA) in delivering support services. By collaborating with national government agencies, local government units, and development partners, RAPID Growth helps facilitate access to technology, financing, market opportunities, and business development services needed to scale agro-enterprises and empower rural communities.
