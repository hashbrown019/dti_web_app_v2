Beneficiaries

The RAPID Growth Project focuses on empowering key actors in agricultural value chains who play an essential role in rural economic development. By strengthening their capacities, improving market access, and supporting enterprise growth, the project helps ensure that the benefits of agro-enterprise development reach communities that need them most.

The project works with the following groups:

  • Smallholder farmers

    Smallholder farmers form the backbone of agricultural production in many rural communities. The project supports farmers by strengthening their capacity to improve productivity, adopt better farming practices, and connect with reliable markets. Through stronger value chain linkages, farmers gain better opportunities to increase income and participate more actively in agro-enterprise development.

  • Farmer organizations

    Farmer organizations, including cooperatives and associations, play a vital role in organizing producers, strengthening bargaining power, and improving access to services and markets. The project supports these organizations in enhancing their management capabilities, strengthening collective marketing, and building sustainable partnerships with agro-enterprises and market actors.

  • Micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs)

    Agri-based MSMEs serve as important drivers of rural economic growth by processing agricultural products, creating employment, and expanding market opportunities for farmers. The project works with MSMEs to strengthen their competitiveness through improved business practices, technology adoption, and better integration within agricultural value chains.

  • Special sectors

    The RAPID Growth Project promotes inclusive development by supporting the participation of women, youth, and Indigenous Peoples in agro-enterprises and value chains. The project helps expand their access to livelihood opportunities, enterprise development, and capacity-building initiatives to ensure that rural economic growth benefits diverse communities.
