Our Goals

The RAPID Growth Project aims to contribute to the reduction of poverty incidence in rural areas by supporting sustained income growth among smallholder farmers, women, youth, and Indigenous Peoples (IPs) across selected agri-based value chains in the Philippines

To achieve this, the project promotes collaborative value chain development, strengthening commercial partnerships between producers, agro-enterprises, and market actors. Through coordinated action plans and strategic investments, the RAPID Growth Project helps create stronger backward linkages between agri-based MSMEs and farming communities.
