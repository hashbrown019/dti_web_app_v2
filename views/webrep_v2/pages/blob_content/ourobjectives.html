Our Objectives

The RAPID Growth Project aims to create enabling conditions for the sustained growth of smallholder farmers and micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) within selected agricultural value chains.

The project focuses on strengthening the competitiveness and market participation of stakeholders in the cacao, coconut, coffee, and processed fruits and nuts value chains, particularly banana cardava and calamansi, across Eastern Visayas and Mindanao.

Through targeted interventions and strategic partnerships, the project supports value chain development by improving market access, strengthening enterprise capacity, and facilitating access to technology, financing, and business development services.
